### Option 1: ngrok (Recommended)
**Best for: Most reliable, HTTPS support**

2. **Sign up**: Create free account at [ngrok.com](https://ngrok.com)

## ğŸ› Troubleshooting

### ngrok Issues:
- **"not authenticated"**: Run `ngrok config add-authtoken YOUR_TOKEN`
- **"tunnel not found"**: Check your ngrok account plan limits
- **Connection refused**: Make sure your Flask app is running first

### General Issues:
- **Tunnel not working**: Check your firewall settings
- **Slow response**: Try a different tunnel provider
- **URL expired**: Some services rotate URLs, check your tunnel dashboard

### VRChat OSC Issues:
- **Avatar not responding**: Enable OSC in VRChat settings
- **Parameters not working**: Make sure parameters are in your avatar AND Unity project
- **Delayed response**: Normal, OSC has slight latency
